Retirement Calculator
Inputs:

-Current age
-Retirement age

Outputs:

-Number of years left until retirement
-Year the user can retire

Constraints:

-Current age and retirement age must be non-negative.
Retirement age must be greater than or equal to current age.

Edge cases:

-If the user enters a negative age or a retirement age less than the current age, the program should display an error message.

Algorithm:

-Validate the input.
-Calculate the number of years left until retirement by subtracting the current age from the retirement age.
-Calculate the year the user can retire by adding the number of years left until retirement to the current year.
-Display the results to the user.
